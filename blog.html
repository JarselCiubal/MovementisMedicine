Starting Your Journey to Movement from Ground Zero

If you're starting from zero, don’t worry—you’re not alone. Here are some simple ways to ease into a more active lifestyle:

  • Daily Walks: Start with 5-10 minutes of walking around your neighborhood.
  • Stretching: Spend 5-10 minutes every morning stretching.
  • Chair Exercises: Incorporate simple seated exercises.
  • Set Small Goals: Aim for achievable milestones.

Beginner-Friendly Exercises

  • Bodyweight Squats
  • Wall Push-ups
  • Standing Marches
  • Gentle Yoga or Stretching Videos

Explore Different Activities

It’s essential to find an activity you enjoy so that staying active feels fun rather than a chore.
